Welcome to Atom's Adventure! You play as a blue particle that wanders around the night (controlled by mouse) and your job is to collect purple mana orbs that fall from the sky. At the same time, you must avoid red particles and collect mana before the timer (located on the top left) runs out. Every mana orb you collect will allow you to reset the timer. The more mana you get, the higher your score (located on the top left). The higher your score, the more customization options you will unlock. Have fun! :D
